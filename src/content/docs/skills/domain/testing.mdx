---
title: Testing Skill
description: Domain skill for testing with Vitest and Playwright.
---

The testing skill gives Claude deep knowledge of testing patterns, frameworks, and best practices.

## When It's Active

Activated automatically when `/mykit.init` detects testing frameworks (presence of `vitest.config.*` or `playwright.config.*`).

## What It Teaches Claude

- Vitest unit and integration testing
- Playwright end-to-end testing
- Test structure and organization
- Mocking and stubbing patterns
- Coverage configuration and thresholds
