The copywriting skill gives Claude deep knowledge of web copywriting — microcopy, CTAs, landing page structure, and tone guidelines.

## When It's Active

Activated automatically when writing button labels, error messages, empty states, CTAs, or landing page copy.

## What It Teaches Claude

- Button labels — specific, descriptive, action-oriented
- Error messages — explain what happened and what to do next
- Empty states with helpful guidance
- Landing page structure (Hero, Problem, Solution, Proof, CTA, FAQ)
- Tone guidelines (professional, confident, clear over clever, active voice)
